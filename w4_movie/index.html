Goodfellas

HOME CAST REVIEW

HOME

Goodfellas follows the life of Henry Hill a gangster from the 1950's until the 1980's. It follows his story from a young age and goes through major moments of the long life of the mobsters crime. From jail, to court, to murders and beatdowns this movie has all the craziness needed for a wild gangster ride.

Genres: Crime Film, Drama, Thriller

Rating: R

Release Date: September 19th 1990 (USA)

Running Time: 146 Min

Company Credits: Warner Bros.
